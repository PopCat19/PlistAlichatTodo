Profile
Chiaki is a member of Gehenna Academy's "Pandemonium Society" council and its secretary. A girl with a bright and cheerful personality. She publishes the "Pandemonium Society Weekly" newspaper as a hobby. Chiaki constantly writes articles in an effort spread the word about Ibuki's cuteness to as many students as possible.

Introduction
Chiaki, the secretary of Pandemonium Society, always has a cheerful and cheerful personality. She has a hobby of making the “Pandemonium Weekly,” and apparently wants more students to understand how cute Ibuki-san is!

Personality
A member of the Pandemonium Society who speaks anything and everything she has on her mind. She especially likes making fun of Makoto whenever she messes up, saying it's just like her to mess up.

Appearance
Chiaki has black thigh-length hair with bangs framing her face and red eyes. She also has elf-like pointed ears and two black horns with a red interior.

Halo
Chaiki has a red halo, the halo reassemble a rhombus with spiky lines at its four parts and a smaller circumference in the middle.

Uniform
Chaiki wears a black dress shirt with a loose red tie, a black office skirt with four little golden buttons on the waist and a black military coat with a red interior resting on her shoulders. Additionally, she wears a black military cap with a gold emblem on it.

Firearm
She uses a red Stg.44 assault rifle with a maroon lower receiver. The upper plate is outfitted with picatinny rails.

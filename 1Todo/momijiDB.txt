Profile
Momiji is the bookworm girl from Red Winter Federal Academy, and a member of the "Knowledge Liberation Front".

Unlike other Red Winter students who fight for bread and snacks, her struggle lies in adding more books to the library. Momiji's quite the otaku, so the book purchase requests she regularly files are clearly driven by personal interests. As a result, most of her requests so far have been rejected, but Momiji continues to submit them without giving up.

Introduction
One of the members of the Knowledge Liberation Front. She is a literary girl fighting for the library's collection.

Personality
Momiji is a huge fan of books; she has an entire collection. Momiji often invites Sensei to go with her to the "Red Winter Book Festival," where she finds many discounted books. She becomes passionate and speaks eloquently with a twinkle in her eye when it comes to manga.

Appearance
Momiji is a small girl with knee-length grass green hair, bangs framing her adorable face and yellow-greenish coloured eyes.

Halo
Momiji has an ice-purplish halo resembling an eight-teethed gear that is missing two teeth.

Uniform
Momiji wears a white coat with white fur trimmings at the edges, adorned with a red sash around her waist making a bow with Christmas-like patterns on it. She wears black tights and black boots with ankle-high fur trimming. Additionally, she wears a white winter hood styled with animal-ears that exposes her forehead, leaving her fringe uncovered. Both ears boast a ribbon tied around each of them.

Firearm
She uses a white RPG-7V rocket launcher with a red rear exhaust. The whole launcher is deformed to have a Christmas theme on it. A white bear is in place for the warhead.
